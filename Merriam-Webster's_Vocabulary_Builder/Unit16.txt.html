OMNI meaning "all." In English words, omni- can mean "in all way," "in all place," or "without limits." An omnidirectional antenna, for example, is one that receives or sends radio waves equally well in all directions. And Omni by itself has been used repeatedly as a brand name for things as different as a hotel chain and a science magazine.
